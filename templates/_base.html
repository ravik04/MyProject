Who Are WE?

IIT Delhi's Office of Career Services (OCS) is a facilitating body conducting training and placement activities for the brightest young minds of the country. IIT Delhi believes in creating professionals with a sense of integrity, awareness & excellence. We are committed to bridge the gap between these students and the world outside their campus to be able to pursue their dreams. Apart from providing job and internship opportunities, OCS also offers guidance to students that helps them shape their career through various workshops and industrial tours
